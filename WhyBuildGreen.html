Why build green?

    • Green buildings outperform nongreen buildings.

    • % of execs agreeing, Turner Construction Survey, Sustainable Commercial Interiors, Penny Bonda and Katie Sosnowchik, 2007

    • Recruitment and retention are increasingly important today.

      Going green gives you a competitive edge.
      “Workers today want purpose and meaning in their work; younger workers, in particular, know a great deal about sustainability. Amidst a talent shortage, individuals are looking for employers that “get it” and care about sustainability. Thus, an organization’s approach to sustainability will make a difference in its ability to attract, engage and retain employees”
    • — SHRM 2008 Exec. Roundtable Symposium on Sustainability and HR Management Strategy

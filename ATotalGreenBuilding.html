Why build green?

    • Energy Use
      • Energy conservation; renewable energy systems, reduced use of ozone-depleting chemicals in HVAC systems, green power use
      • ENERGY STAR-rated appliances such as copiers, computer monitors, printers
      • High-efficiency lighting
      • Occupancy sensors
    • Water efficiency/conservation
      • Bathroom and kitchen fixtures
      • Materials and resource conservation
      • Use of salvaged materials, recycled content materials, regionally produced materials, agricultural-based materials and certified wood products
    • Indoor environmental quality
      • Improved ventilation and indoor air quality
      • Use of nontoxic finishes, paints, flooring and furniture
      • Daylighting and views to the outdoors
      • Thermal comfort
      • Individual control of lighting and HVAC system

“The whole is greater than the sum of its parts”— Gestalt theory
When you take a serious look at the environment your employees work in day in and day out, optimizing that workspace can catapult performance.
No one looks at commercial interiors like EGG. Our approach to commercial interiors yields so much more because it’s three-pronged. We combine ergonomics, sustainability and aesthetics into our planning and design.

  • ERGONOMIC

    • Ergonomics focuses on designing workstation and workflow for safety, efficiency and comfort. Effective ergonomic design reduces discomfort and injuries and increases job satisfaction and productivity.
    • EGG looks at how your employees can best function in the workspace and makes recommendations for everything from furniture, lighting, surfaces, materials, and office space layout and resulting workflow.

  • GREEN

    • Sustainability is a huge factor in recruiting and engaging employees. Our offerings include energy saving solutions, indoor environment and air quality improvements, Total Green Building recommendations, and environmentally-friendly furniture and materials.
    • EGG is a USGBC member and holds a Commercial Certification with Green Building Advantage.

    • A word about sustainability

      “Green buildings can do more to mitigate climate change than all the curbs on greenhouse gases stipulated under the Kyoto protocol, while saving billions of dollars”
      —UN Survey

  • GOOD

    • Aesthetics are a key driver of every plan and recommendation we make.
    • We understand employees function better in positive spaces. When they feel better about their workspace, they work more, work better and are more likely to remain in your employ.
